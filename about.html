OUR STORY

How The Find Lab Started

In 2024, we noticed a big problem in the local market: people were buying “good deals” that turned into headaches — wrong specs, hidden issues, and no support after purchase.

2024: The beginning

We started small — sourcing business-grade devices, testing them properly, and only listing what we would confidently use ourselves. The goal was simple: reliable tech, fair pricing, and real support.

  • Quality checks and honest condition grading
  • Clean installs + updates
  • Clear communication with buyers

Trust first

Our customers kept coming back because we didn’t treat it like “just a sale”. We treated it like someone’s work setup, school device, or office rollout.

  • No vague specs
  • Business-class models (Latitude / EliteBook / ThinkPad)
  • Support when something goes wrong

From devices to setups

As demand grew, we moved into office setups: ultrawide monitors, deskside installs, cabling, and team deployments.

  • 34” / 49” ultrawide deployments
  • Docking + multi-monitor configurations
  • Small-team rollout planning

Where we’re going

The next phase is simple: become the go-to local supplier for growing teams across the GTA — with fast availability, bulk pricing, and support plans.

  • Business supply + repeat orders
  • Inventory drops weekly
  • Optional monthly support

DATA SECURITY & DEVICE CERTIFICATION

Safety. Security. Professional Standards.

Every device that enters The Find Lab goes through professional-grade data destruction, hardware inspection, and certified system installation before reaching our customers.

Secure Data Destruction

All storage drives are wiped using professional-grade data destruction software to ensure previous company or user data is permanently removed.

  • Multi-pass secure wipe process
  • Protection against data recovery
  • Suitable for business environments

Fresh Genuine Windows Installation

After wiping, all machines receive a clean installation of genuine Windows with latest updates and drivers installed.

  • Activated original-grade Windows
  • Clean install — no leftover data
  • Fully updated & optimized

Professional Hardware Testing

Every unit is inspected using professional diagnostic software before approval.

  • RAM health testing
  • Battery cycle & health inspection
  • SSD/HDD health verification
  • Thermal & performance checks

Business-Ready Certification

Devices are only listed once they pass inspection standards suitable for office and professional use.

  • Performance validated
  • Cleaned & sanitized
  • Ready for deployment
