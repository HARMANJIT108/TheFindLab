FAST FIXES

Try these first (2 minutes)

If it still doesn’t work, send a support request — we’ll take it from there.

1) Restart properly

Shut down fully, wait 10 seconds, then power back on.

2) Check power & cables

Try a different outlet/cable. For monitors, reseat HDMI/DP.

3) Note the symptoms

What happens exactly? Any error message? When did it start?

Support FAQ

How do I claim warranty/coverage?

Send your invoice number (or purchase date), device model, and the issue. We’ll confirm next steps fast.

Do you offer onsite support in the GTA?

Yes for business/team setups and some cases. We’ll confirm availability based on location and urgency.

Can you help with data transfer?

Yes. Tell us old device + new device model and what data you need moved (files, browser, email).

What info should I send for faster help?

Invoice/order info, device model, serial (if available), and a short video/photo of the issue.
